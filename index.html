I am currently employed as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET), Bangladesh from 2022. I have graduated from KUET with a B.SC in CSE, earning a CGPA of 3.96 out of 4.0 and achieving the first position out of 121. Aside, I am preparing myself for my Master's where i am working on building an Adaptive Recommender Systems based on incremental matrix factorization under the supersion of Dr. K. M. Azharul Hasan. Wishing to learn more about the dicoveries of AI, I have actively been working on my projects collaborately. Moreover, I love to teach my students what i know and try to guide them in real-life based problems focusing on healthcare and child safety.

Research Interests: Digital Health, Human-Computer Interaction, Image Processing And Computer Vision, Recommender Systems, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning.

Besides academic life, I love painting, watching movies and TV series—especially in the action, sci-fi and thriller genres. My all time favourite is Spider-man, being a big fan of Marvel movies and shows.
